## Sending Guidelines

- All emails sent via SendGrid from contact@neshama.ca
- One at a time — never bulk. Each email is personalized.
- Grief test before every send: "Would this feel okay the week your parent died?"
- No marketing jargon. No hype. No "limited time offer" language.
- Follow up once or twice, then stop. Respect silence.
