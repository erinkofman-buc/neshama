## TALKING POINTS (if Jordana gets questions)

**"Is it really free?"**
Yes. No accounts, no fees, no ads. Erin built it as a community resource.

**"Who built this?"**
Erin Kofman — she's a pharmacist in Toronto. She built it because she saw how hard it is for families to coordinate everything when someone passes.

**"Is it only Orthodox / only kosher?"**
No — it serves the entire Jewish community. All denominations. The caterer directory has all types of food — kosher is a filter, not the category.

**"Why Toronto and Montreal?"**
Those are the two largest Jewish communities in Canada. She started with the funeral homes that serve them.

**"How many vendors are on there?"**
Over 100 food and gift vendors. Everything from kosher caterers to Mediterranean, Italian, sushi — plus gift baskets, flowers, and sympathy packages.

**"Can I add my caterer / food business?"**
Yes — there's an application form at neshama.ca/shiva/caterers or email contact@neshama.ca.

**"Is it safe / legit?"**
Yes. All obituary data comes directly from the funeral home websites. No personal data is collected. No accounts needed.
