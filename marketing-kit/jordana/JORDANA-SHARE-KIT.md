## TALKING POINTS (when people ask)

**"Is it really free?"**
Yes. No accounts, no fees, no ads.

**"Who built this?"**
My friend Erin Kofman. She's a pharmacist in Toronto. She built it because she saw how hard it is for families to pull everything together when someone passes.

**"Is it only for Orthodox families?"**
No — it's for the whole community. All denominations. Kosher is a filter, not the default.

**"Why Toronto and Montreal?"**
Those are the biggest Jewish communities in Canada. That's where the funeral homes are.

**"Can my caterer get listed?"**
Yes — neshama.ca/shiva/caterers has an application form. Or just email contact@neshama.ca.

**"Is it legit?"**
All the obituary info comes straight from the funeral home websites. Nothing weird, nothing hidden. No data collected.

**"What about Passover?"**
There's actually a whole page about navigating your first Passover after a loss — neshama.ca/first-passover-after-loss. Really gentle, really thoughtful. Worth sharing with anyone who might be dreading the seder this year.
