Dear Benjamin's Park Memorial Chapel team,

My name is Erin Kofman. I built a free community resource called Neshama (neshama.ca) for Jewish families in Toronto and Montreal, and I wanted to introduce myself.

Neshama aggregates obituaries from the Jewish funeral homes in our community — including Benjamin's Park — so families don't have to check multiple websites when someone passes away. One search brings up everything. Hundreds of community members already visit each week to find listings, coordinate meals for shiva, and figure out how to help.

We're officially launching on March 24, and as we prepare, I wanted to make sure the funeral homes whose listings are at the heart of Neshama know about us — and have a voice in how we move forward.

**Why I'm reaching out:**

Every Benjamin's listing on Neshama links directly back to your original page. We're not redirecting families away from you — we're helping more people find you. But I wanted you to know we exist, and I wanted to give you a voice in how your listings appear.

If you'd prefer a different format, want your logo included, or would rather provide a direct data feed — I'd welcome that.

**What Neshama does beyond obituaries:**

- **Meal coordination for shiva** — families set up a page, volunteers sign up for specific days. Everyone sees what's covered and what's still needed, so nothing falls through the cracks.
- **"How Can I Help?" hub** — 120+ local food and gift vendors for people who want to send something meaningful.
- **Yahrzeit reminders** — annual email reminders based on the Hebrew calendar, sent before the date so families have time to prepare.
- **Condolence guestbook** — every memorial page includes a place for messages of comfort.

**Free. No ads. No accounts.** Neshama exists to serve the community — the same families you serve every day.

I think there's a natural fit here. If there's a way Neshama could be useful to your families — a link on your resources page, a mention during arrangements, a printed card for the chapel — I'd be grateful for the partnership. And if anything about your listings needs adjusting, just let me know.

Happy to meet in person or hop on a call anytime.
