# Vendor Outreach Email Template
Updated: March 1, 2026

Personalize for each vendor. Replace variables before sending.

**Variables:**
- `{{vendor_name}}` — Business name (e.g., "Tov Li Catering")
- `{{category}}` — Their category (e.g., "Kosher Caterers", "Gift Baskets")
- `{{city}}` — Toronto or Montreal
- `{{listing_url}}` — Their listing page (e.g., neshama.ca/help/food/tov-li-catering)

---

## Subject Line Options

1. Your {{category}} listing on Neshama.ca
2. {{vendor_name}} is listed on Neshama — a resource for Jewish families
3. You're part of something meaningful — Neshama.ca

---

## Email Body

Hi there,

My name is Erin Kofman. I built Neshama (neshama.ca) — a resource for Jewish families in {{city}} dealing with a loss.

When someone in our community passes away, the people around them want to help. They want to send a meal, bring food for shiva, or find something meaningful to send. Neshama helps them do that by bringing everything into one place: obituaries from local funeral homes, a meal coordination tool, and a directory of trusted local vendors like you.

**{{vendor_name}} is already listed on Neshama.** You can see your listing here: {{listing_url}}

I included your business because I believe you offer something families and their supporters genuinely need during a difficult time. Your listing is live now, and people in the community are already using the directory to find vendors for shiva meals, sympathy gifts, and other ways to show support.

Here is what your listing includes:
- Your business name, contact information, and a link to your website
- Your category ({{category}}) so families can find you when they need you
- A description of what you offer

**If anything on your listing needs updating** — hours, description, contact details, photos — just reply to this email and I will take care of it.

---

**One more thing: Featured Vendor**

Some vendors have asked about standing out in the directory. I have created a Featured Vendor option that places your listing at the top of your category with a highlighted badge and expanded profile. The founding rate is **$49/month**, and it is locked at that price for as long as you keep it.

This is entirely optional. Your current listing is included at no cost and will remain that way. Featured Vendor is simply there for businesses that want more visibility.

If you are interested or have questions, just reply to this email.

Thank you for being part of what families rely on during the hardest moments.

Warm regards,
Erin Kofman
neshama.ca
contact@neshama.ca

## Notes for Sending

- Send from contact@neshama.ca via SendGrid
- BCC yourself on every send
- Personalize the listing URL — confirm it loads before sending
- If vendor is a caterer, use "shiva meals" language; if gift vendor, use "sympathy gifts" language
- Do NOT send in bulk. These go out one at a time, personalized.
- Follow up in 5-7 days if no response: "Hi — just checking that you received my note about your listing on Neshama. Happy to update anything or answer questions."
