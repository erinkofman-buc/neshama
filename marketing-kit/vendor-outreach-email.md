# Vendor Outreach Email Template
Updated: March 13, 2026

Personalize for each vendor. Replace variables before sending.

**Variables:**
- `{{vendor_name}}` — Business name
- `{{category}}` — Their category (e.g., "Kosher Caterers", "Gift Baskets")
- `{{city}}` — Toronto or Montreal
- `{{listing_url}}` — Their listing page (confirm it loads before sending)

---

## Subject Line Options

1. {{vendor_name}} is listed on Neshama — a resource for Jewish families
2. Your {{category}} listing on Neshama.ca
3. You're part of something meaningful — Neshama.ca

---

## Email Body

Hi there,

My name is Erin Kofman. I built Neshama (neshama.ca) — a resource for Jewish families in {{city}} dealing with a loss.

When someone passes away, the people around them want to help. Send a meal. Bring food for shiva. Find something meaningful to send. Neshama helps them do that — obituaries from the public funeral home sites, a meal coordination tool, and a directory of local vendors like you.

**{{vendor_name}} is already listed on Neshama.** Your listing: {{listing_url}}

I included you because what you offer is exactly what families and their supporters look for during a difficult time. The listing is live, and people are using the directory to find vendors for shiva meals, gifts, and other ways to show support.

Your listing includes:
- Business name, contact info, and a link to your website
- Your category ({{category}}) so families find you when they need you
- A description of what you offer

**If anything needs updating** — hours, description, contact details, photos — just reply and I'll take care of it.

---

**One more thing: Featured Vendor**

Some vendors have asked about more visibility. Featured Vendor places your listing at the top of your category with a highlighted badge and expanded profile. Founding rate: **$49/month**, locked at that price for as long as you keep it.

Totally optional. Your current listing is free and stays that way. Featured is just there for businesses that want to stand out.

Interested or have questions? Just reply.

Thank you for being part of what families rely on during the hardest moments.

Warm regards,
Erin Kofman
neshama.ca
contact@neshama.ca

P.S. The $49/month founding rate is only available now. When I raise the price for new vendors, yours stays locked. No contracts — cancel anytime.

---

## Notes for Sending

- Send from contact@neshama.ca via SendGrid
- BCC yourself on every send
- Confirm the listing URL loads before sending
- Caterers → "shiva meals" language. Gift vendors → "sympathy gifts" language.
- One at a time. Never bulk.
- Follow up in 5-7 days: "Hi — just checking you got my note about your listing on Neshama. Happy to update anything or answer questions."
