Dear Holy Blossom team,

My name is Erin Kofman. I'm a licensed pharmacist in Toronto, and I built a free resource called Neshama (neshama.ca) for Jewish families dealing with a loss.

Neshama brings together obituaries from Toronto and Montreal funeral homes into one searchable feed — Steeles Memorial, Benjamin's Park, Paperman & Sons, and Misaskim. It also includes a meal coordination tool for shiva, so volunteers can sign up for specific nights without overlap, and a directory of 120+ local food and gift vendors.

Everything is free. There are no ads on obituary pages and no paywalls on any of the core tools.

I'm reaching out because I think this could be a helpful resource to share with congregants when they're supporting a family through a loss. If you'd like, I can send a short description suitable for a newsletter or bulletin.

Thank you for the work you do for Toronto's Jewish community.

Warm regards,
