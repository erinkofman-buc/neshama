# Neshama Demo Video Script

**Total time:** 60–90 seconds
**Format:** Screen recording of neshama.ca with voiceover
**Who records:** Erin or Jordana — phone propped up, screen record the site on laptop/phone
**Share on:** WhatsApp (as video file), Instagram Reels, site at /demo

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:10] Hook
**Show:** neshama.ca landing page (scroll slowly past hero)
**Say:** "When someone in our community passes away, everyone wants to help — but no one knows where to start. That's why we built Neshama."

### [0:10–0:20] The Feed
**Show:** Click "Listings" → scroll the obituary feed, type a name in search
**Say:** "Neshama pulls obituaries from Toronto and Montreal's Jewish funeral homes into one place. Updated daily. Search by name."

### [0:20–0:35] How Can I Help?
**Show:** Click "How Can I Help?" → show the 3 cards → click "Bring or Order Food" → scroll vendor list
**Say:** "See someone you know? Click 'How Can I Help' — find local kosher caterers, send a gift basket, or get a preparation checklist. Over a hundred trusted vendors."

### [0:35–0:55] Organize Meals
**Show:** Click "Organize Shiva" → show step 1 (your info) → step 2 (shiva details, fill in a couple fields) → step 3 (review) → the success screen with share buttons
**Say:** "If you're the friend coordinating meals — it takes three minutes. Add the family's details, set the dates, share the link on WhatsApp. Friends sign up for a day. No accounts needed."

### [0:55–1:10] The Calendar
**Show:** Open a shiva page → scroll to meal calendar (green/yellow/red) → show volunteer names
**Say:** "The meal calendar shows what's covered and what still needs help. Green means covered. Yellow means almost. The family sees it all at a glance — no duplicate casseroles."

### [1:10–1:20] Close
**Show:** Scroll back to landing page hero
**Say:** "Neshama. Every soul remembered, every family supported. Try it at neshama.ca — and share it with someone who needs it."

---

## Recording Tips

1. **Use your phone in landscape** if recording for Instagram Reels/Stories (or portrait for WhatsApp)
2. **Record the laptop screen** — just navigate the live site slowly while talking
3. **One take is fine** — imperfect feels authentic for community sharing
4. **Background:** quiet room, no music needed
5. **Length:** aim for 60 seconds, can stretch to 90 if natural
6. **If you stumble:** just keep going, it's not a commercial

## Quick Version (30 seconds — WhatsApp/Story)

**Show:** Landing → Feed → "How Can I Help?" cards → Meal calendar (green/yellow/red)
**Say:** "Neshama brings together obituaries and shiva meal coordination for the Toronto and Montreal Jewish community. Find who you're looking for, organize meals in three minutes, and share the link. No accounts, no fees — just community. Neshama.ca."
