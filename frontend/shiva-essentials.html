What the Funeral Home Provides

Most of these are included in the funeral home's service. Call them before buying anything on this list — they handle more than most families expect.

Memorial Candle The ner neshama — burns for seven days
Shiva Chairs Low seating for the mourners
Siddurim Prayer books for minyan services
Kippot Head coverings for visitors
Tehillim Book of Psalms for comfort
Shiva Notice Dates, times, and address published
Tip: The funeral home usually handles these as part of their service. If you're not sure what's included, just ask — they're there to help.
