What Featured placement is

When a family coordinates a shiva through Neshama, they browse a directory of local caterers and meal providers. Featured placement lists your business first, above the rest of the directory, so high-intent families see you at the moment they are deciding who to call.

Families never pay, and they never see pricing, sponsorship tags, or advertising. To a family, the directory simply reads as a trusted list. Featured is a quiet ordering preference on the vendor side, nothing more.

    Terms and billing details

    Neshama's role. Neshama operates a directory that helps families find local caterers, meal providers, and related businesses. Listings and Featured placements are provided for discovery only. Neshama does not prepare, sell, deliver, or guarantee any vendor's food, products, or services, and is not a party to any arrangement between a family and a vendor. Each vendor is solely responsible for its own offerings, including food safety, allergen information, kashrut or other dietary claims, pricing, fulfilment, and compliance with applicable law. Families deal with vendors directly and at their own discretion. Featured placement reflects a paid arrangement with the vendor and is never represented to families as an endorsement.

    Pricing. Featured placement is $49 CAD per month. Prices are in Canadian dollars, and tax is not currently charged.

    Billing and cancellation. Your placement begins with a 90-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial, and we will email you before the first charge so you can cancel beforehand if you wish. You can cancel any time from the billing portal. Cancellation stops future charges and ends Featured placement at the end of the current paid period. If you have already been charged for the current month, contact us at contact@neshama.ca and we will refund that month on request.
