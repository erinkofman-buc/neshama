The Meaning of Neshama

Neshama (נשמה) is the Hebrew word for "soul" — the divine spark within each person. This service was created to ensure that every soul in Toronto's Jewish community is remembered with the dignity and respect they deserve. We believe that honouring those who have passed is one of the greatest acts of kindness we can offer.
