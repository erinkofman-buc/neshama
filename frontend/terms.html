1. About Neshama

Neshama (“the Service”) is an obituary aggregation and shiva coordination platform operated from Ontario, Canada. We gather publicly available obituary listings from Jewish funeral homes in the Toronto and Montreal areas, and provide tools to help communities support one another during times of loss.

Our services include:

  • Aggregated obituary listings from partner funeral homes
  • Memorial pages with community tributes and guestbook entries
  • Shiva meal coordination tools for families and volunteers
  • A vendor directory of caterers and service providers
  • Curated guides and resources for families in mourning
  • Daily email digests of recent obituary listings
