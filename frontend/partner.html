What We Do

  • Obituary Aggregation We collect listings from Jewish funeral homes into one respectful, searchable feed — updated daily.
  • Shiva Meal Coordination Friends and community members sign up to bring meals — no duplicate dishes, no missed nights.
  • Vendor Directory Caterers, gift baskets, memorial items — a curated directory of community-trusted providers.
  • Yahrzeit Reminders Annual remembrance notifications so no anniversary of loss passes unnoticed.

How We Work Together

  • 1
    Funeral Homes Your listings reach the broader community automatically. Families find funeral and shiva details in one place, alongside practical support.
  • 2
    Community Organisations Share Neshama with your members so they never miss a chance to attend a funeral, visit a shiva, or send a meal. We make chesed easier.
  • 3
    Chesed & Bikur Cholim Groups Coordinate shiva meals through our free organiser tool. No more group chats, spreadsheets, or missed nights.
  • 4
    Synagogues & Rabbis Point congregants to Neshama for yahrzeit reminders and community-wide obituary awareness. We complement your existing communications.
