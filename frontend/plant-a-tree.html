Plant a Tree in Israel

A living memorial through the Jewish National Fund

A Tradition of Remembrance

Planting a tree in Israel is one of the most meaningful ways to honour a loved one's memory. Through the Jewish National Fund (JNF), your tree becomes part of the land of Israel, growing for generations to come.

When you plant a tree, a beautiful certificate is sent to the mourning family, letting them know that a living memorial has been created in their loved one's name.

How It Works

  • Visit the JNF website and choose to plant a tree in memory
  • Enter the details of the person being honoured
  • Provide the family's address for the certificate
  • JNF sends a beautiful certificate to the family
  • Your tree is planted in one of JNF's forests in Israel

Why It Matters

Trees planted through JNF contribute to reforestation, environmental sustainability, and the greening of the Negev. Each tree is a symbol of life, growth, and hope â€” values that resonate deeply in times of loss.

This gift is appropriate for anyone, regardless of their level of observance. It's universally appreciated and carries deep meaning within the Jewish tradition.
