## How to schedule each post (30 seconds each):
1. Click **Create post** in Meta Business Suite
2. Click **Add photo/video** → select the files listed above
3. Paste the caption into the **Text** box
4. Click the arrow next to **Publish** → **Schedule** → set the date/time
5. Click **Schedule**
